# Statement of Work (SOW) #01
## Product Overview & Objectives

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** November 24, 2025  
**Project:** Instagram Photo Editing App (Open Source)

## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the product vision, objectives, and scope for an **open-source mobile photo-editing application** specifically designed for Instagram content creators. The app aims to provide users with powerful, intuitive editing tools to enhance their photos before posting to Instagram.

---

## 2. Project Vision

### 2.1 Mission Statement
To create a **free, open-source, and user-friendly photo-editing mobile application** that empowers Instagram users to create professional-quality content using market-recommended tools and best practices from leading photo editing apps.

### 2.2 Core Values
- **Open Source:** Fully transparent, community-driven development
- **User-Centric:** Easy-to-use interface designed for Instagram creators
- **Feature-Rich:** Incorporating best features from top photo editing apps
- **Performance:** Fast, responsive, and lightweight
- **Cross-Platform:** Available on both iOS and Android

---

## 3. Product Overview

### 3.1 Product Description
A mobile application that provides comprehensive photo editing capabilities optimized for Instagram's format requirements (1:1, 4:5, 16:9, 9:16 aspect ratios). The app will feature:

- **Advanced Filters & Effects:** Instagram-style filters plus professional-grade adjustments
- **Smart Editing Tools:** AI-powered enhancements, background removal, object selection
- **Text & Graphics:** Typography tools, stickers, and overlays
- **Batch Processing:** Edit multiple photos with consistent settings
- **Export Optimization:** Instagram-ready exports with optimal resolution and compression

### 3.2 Target Audience
- Instagram content creators (influencers, businesses, hobbyists)
- Social media managers
- Photographers seeking quick mobile edits
- Users seeking free alternatives to premium photo editing apps
- Open-source contributors and developers

---

## 4. Project Objectives

### 4.1 Primary Objectives
1. **Deliver a functional MVP** with core editing features within 3-4 months
2. **Build an active open-source community** with clear contribution guidelines
3. **Achieve 10,000+ downloads** within 6 months of launch
4. **Maintain 4.0+ star rating** on app stores
5. **Establish consistent release cycle** (monthly updates)

### 4.2 Secondary Objectives
1. Integration with Instagram API (if available)
2. Cloud storage for project files
3. Cross-device synchronization
4. Community preset/filter marketplace
5. Multi-language support (10+ languages)

## 6. Product Scope

### 6.1 In-Scope
✅ Core photo editing tools (crop, rotate, adjust, filters)  
✅ Text and sticker overlays  
✅ Instagram aspect ratio presets  
✅ Export optimization for Instagram  
✅ Basic AI-powered features (auto-enhance, background blur)  
✅ Filter library  
✅ Undo/redo functionality  
✅ Native mobile app (iOS & Android)  

### 6.2 Out of Scope (Future Releases)
❌ Video editing capabilities  
❌ Direct Instagram posting (API limitations)  
❌ Desktop application  
❌ Advanced AI features (style transfer, deep learning filters)  
❌ Cloud storage/sync (v2.0 feature)  
❌ Social features (commenting, sharing within app)  

---

## 7. Constraints & Assumptions

### 7.1 Constraints
- **Budget:** Zero budget - fully volunteer/community-driven
- **Instagram API:** Limited or no access to official Instagram API
- **App Store Fees:** $99/year (iOS), $25 one-time (Android)
- **Development Time:** Part-time contributors
- **Open Source License:** MIT License (permissive)

### 7.2 Assumptions
- Community contributors will actively participate
- Users are willing to manually export and upload to Instagram
- Market demand exists for free Instagram editing tools
- App store approval will be obtained
- No legal issues with Instagram trademark usage

---

## 8. Dependencies

1. **Open Source Libraries:** Availability and compatibility of image processing libraries
2. **Platform SDKs:** Flutter/React Native/Native SDK updates
3. **Community Engagement:** Active contributors and maintainers
4. **App Store Policies:** Compliance with iOS and Android guidelines
5. **Third-Party APIs:** For optional features (stock photos, AI services)

---

## 9. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| Low community adoption | Medium | High | Strong marketing, clear documentation, easy setup |
| App store rejection | Low | High | Follow guidelines strictly, beta testing |
| Performance issues on low-end devices | Medium | Medium | Optimize code, provide quality settings |
| Instagram policy changes | Low | Medium | Monitor ToS, avoid direct integration |
| Lack of contributors | Medium | High | Welcoming community, good first issues |
| Feature creep | High | Medium | Strict MVP definition, phased releases |
